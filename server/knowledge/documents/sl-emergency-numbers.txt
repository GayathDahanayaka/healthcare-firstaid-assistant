SRI LANKA EMERGENCY CONTACT NUMBERS

Suwa Seriya Ambulance Service: 1990
Police Emergency Hotline: 119
Fire and Rescue Service: 110
Sri Lanka Red Cross Society: 011-2691095
National Poisons Information Centre (National Hospital Colombo): 011-2691111
Government Information Center: 1919

Notes:
- 1990 (Suwa Seriya) is the free, 24/7 national ambulance service covering
  most of Sri Lanka and should be the first call in any medical emergency.
- 119 connects to the nearest police station for accidents, violence, or
  situations requiring police presence alongside medical help.
